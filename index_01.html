Leading up to the main event of the CHARLES & KEITH Shibuya
flagship opening party, many fashion tastemakers were sighted on

The sighted style stars were decked out in CHARLES & KEITH’s elegant and sophisticated accessories. Social media personalities Lily May Mac,
Oumayma Elboumeshouli, and Jess Alizzi reached for the L’initial monogram bag to add visual interest to their minimalist outfits in an elegant manner.
